This is a example of Dark Vader for dark mode on websites

Dark Vader is a web framework for easily implementing dark mode into websites, whether your building a new one, or retrofitting an existing website, Dark Vader does it all. All you do is add our button to your website, add our code to the end for your header tag, and setup your dark mode style sheet. This gives you full control over what your dark mode experience is like for users.
